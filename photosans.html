Sans the skeleton
    Endless Sans by Joe Zeng (originally from undertale)

Controls are arrow keys, z to enter/ skip through dialogue, and x to make the dialogue go faster. You will not take damage from the blue bones unless you move.

Song that plays is Megalovania by Tobyfox, and is from the Undertale soundtrack
